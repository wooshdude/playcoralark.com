Developments Galore

Many new buildings have been popping up all over the server. Let’s take a look at whats new in each of the server’s Nations.

Section 8

  • The eyesore of a villager breeder next to the pit has been removed. In its place is a small gazebo that leads underground to a brand new state of the art storage bunker, used by the mayor himself
  • The Wooshco gunpowder farm was demolished as a better one was built in a different location.
  • A store will soon be put in the mall selling gunpowder related goods. Any customers who owned a Wooshco Gunpowder Farm Pass will get 50% off all purchases in this upcoming store
  • A mountain near Section 8’s residential area has been excavated to clear space for a Zoo. The project is already well underway with the Zoo’s border and entrance being completed. Construction8 hopes to complete the project by 8/26, but no official opening date has been solidified

Animal Farm

  • A Villager Zombification room has been constructed across the river from the Pumpkin farm
  • The villagers in the Animal Farm trading hall have begun migrating to the Section 8 mall. Anyone who wants to trade with these villagers should double check which villagers are in which location at a given time.
  • A Bullet Train track has been installed connecting Section 8 and Animal Farm. The only thing is, the track is elevated in the air by several hot air balloons. Passengers will want to take the elevators up to the track to ride the Bullet Train. However the elevators only move up for the moment, so passengers should make sure they have a pair of elytra or some other means to safely get down.

Jellyco

  • The Bee-tel is officially open! All rooms are permanently yours should you choose to purchase one. For businessmen, conference rooms are available to ensure your meetings are not disturbed. Not interested in staying? The Bee-tel is home to more than 50 bees. Come to observe them live their lives, listen to an educational talk, or have a meal at the Honeycomb restaurant. The Bee-tel is the place to bee!
  • A new train line has begun construction between Section 8 and Emerald Town. Jellyco has no estimate for when the track will be functional

Water

  • The main tower has been smoothed out. As a result, it has gained volume from becoming more rectangular
  • A kelp and sugarcane farm has been placed in a smaller water building
  • An automatic cocoa bean farm has been installed in Water’s driest building to date

Emerald Town

  • Emerald Town continues to rapidly develop. It is hard to keep up with everything that is going on! More people will certainly be talking about this town as the Section 8 - Emerald Town railroad gets closer to completion.

We Prefer Bees over Bugs

Now we welcome the player support team to give important information on the many changes to the server on the technical side this week.

This is their report.

Changes

  • Spectator mode
  • All players can now enter spectator mode, albeit a very limited version, using /s. While in spectator mode you will not be able to:
  • Pass through blocks that are not transparent.
  • Travel long distances
  • Added new Data Packs
  • Armor stands can be fully customized
  • Use /trigger as_help for more details
  • Players now drop their head when killed
  • Mobs can be silenced (not like that) by naming them “silence me”
  • Villagers and their workbench will be highlighted with the command /trigger vwh_findStation
  • Wandering traders now sell smaller versions of in-game blocks
  • This change does not affect their default trades, so keep looking for that elusive moss block.
  • Towny
  • Cleaned up messages by removing the [g] and [world]
  • Towns will no longer revert to previous snapshots when unclaimed. (If this happened to you, we’re sorry!)
  • General
  • The world’s view distance was increased from 10 chunks to 12. If it continues to be stable this might be bumped up again.
