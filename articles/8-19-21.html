This Title is Creative

As the server grows, our future plans do too. And as our plans grow, testing them out becomes harder and harder in a survival world. That’s why a superflat creative testing world has been added as an extension of the server. 

Players who wish to teleport to the creative world should use the command

mv tp creative

Players who wish to then return to the survival world should use the command

mv tp world

We ask that when in the creative world, you build far enough from other’s creations and do not alter their creations in any way.

I for one am super excited to see what people start building when they have no limitations. A few people have been using this world already to design something that will be filling the gap left by the excavated mountain in Section 8. Here’s a teaser of what they have built so far. If you are that interested, feel free to see it for yourself in the creative world!
