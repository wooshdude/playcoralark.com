What's all The Buzz About?

Many new buildings have been popping up all over the server. Let’s take a look at what's new in each of the server’s Nations.

Section 8

  • The eyesore of a villager breeder next to the pit has been removed. In its place is a small gazebo that leads underground to a brand new state of the art storage bunker, used by the mayor himself
  • The Wooshco gunpowder farm has been demolished as a better one was built in a different location
    • A store will soon be put in the mall selling gunpowder related goods. Any customers who owned a Wooshco Gunpowder Farm Pass will get 50% off all purchases in this store, opening soon
  • A mountain near Section 8’s residential area has been excavated to clear space for a Zoo. The project is already well underway with the Zoo’s border and entrance being completed. Construction8 hopes to complete the project by 8/26, but no official opening date has been solidified

Animal Farm

  • A Villager Zombification room has been constructed across the river from the Pumpkin farm
  • The villagers in the Animal Farm trading hall have begun migrating to the Section 8 mall. Anyone who wants to trade with these villagers should double check which villagers are in which location at a given time
  • A Bullet Train track has been installed connecting Section 8 and Animal Farm. The only thing is, the track is elevated in the air by several hot air balloons. Passengers will want to take the elevators up to the track to ride the Bullet Train. However the elevators only move up for the moment, so passengers should make sure they have a pair of elytra or some other means to safely return to the surface

Jellyco

  • The Bee-tel is officially open! More on this in the next section.
  • A new train line has begun construction between Section 8 and Emerald Town. Jellyco has no estimate for when the track will be functional

Water

  • The main tower has been smoothed out. As a result, it has increased in size from becoming more rectangular
  • A kelp and sugarcane farm has been placed in a smaller water building
  • An automatic cocoa bean farm has been installed in Water’s driest building to date 

Emerald Town

  • Emerald Town continues to rapidly develop. It is hard to keep up with everything that is going on! More people will certainly be talking about this town as the Section 8 - Emerald Town railroad gets closer to completion.

Join the Hive

You may be wondering why this week is so bee themed, that’s because the Bee-tel has officially opened! We wanted to take this moment to highlight some of the features of this very unique creation. Yesterday, I sat down with the CEO of Jellyco to ask some questions about their latest creation:

    Q: “What inspired you to create a hotel for both bees and players?”

    A: “I felt bad that bees are always crammed into such small places to act as their homes. I wanted to give them a safe, spacious environment that felt like home. Its main purpose is to serve the bees, but players can also come and learn about these spectacular creatures.”

    Q: “What can visitors do at the Bee-tel?”

    A: “It's like a home away from home for people. You can stay in one of the rooms, eat at the Honeycomb, get entertained at the theater, have easy access to the open, outside world and the railroad, rent a conference room for important meetings, and some more activities hopefully coming soon in the future!”

    Q: “Why should people come to the Bee-tel?”

    A: “If you need a vacation or if you are at all interested in learning more about bees, the Bee-tel is a great place to stay or visit.”

    Q: “Anything else you want to say?”

    A: “I hope to see you soon at the Bee-tel!”


We Prefer Bees over Bugs

Now we welcome the player support team to give important information on the many changes to the server on the technical side this week.

This is their report.

Changes

  • Spectator mode
    • All players can now enter spectator mode, albeit a very limited version, using /s. While in spectator mode you will not be able to:
      • Pass through blocks that are not transparent.
      • Travel long distances
  • Added new Data Packs
    • Armor stands can be fully customized
      • Use /trigger as_help for more details
    • Players now drop their head when killed
    • Mobs can be silenced (not like that) by naming them “silence me”
    • Villagers and their assigned workbench will be highlighted with the command /trigger vwh_findStation
    • Wandering traders now sell smaller versions of in-game blocks
      • This change does not affect their default trades, so keep looking for that elusive moss block.
  • Towny
    • Cleaned up messages by removing the [g] and [world]
    • Towns will no longer revert to previous snapshots when unclaimed. (If this happened to you, we’re sorry!)
  • General
    • The world’s view distance was increased from 10 chunks to 12. If it continues to be stable this might be bumped up again.
