This week at Coralark, we return. "What is up with that Coralark word? "What happened to Your Mom SMP?" Some of you may be asking. You will learn more about that a little bit later.

    Symbols and Names

    The server and website has officially gotten a new logo! Along with this we are excited to announce that a name for the geographical world that makes up the server has finally been picked: Coralark! No longer will the land be simply referred to as ‘world’ or ‘ the world’.

    Because of this, we have decided to rename the Minecraft server to simply be Coralark, replacing its old name of Your Mom SMP. Additionally, we will now have all future additions of our weekly news updates under the name of “This Week at Coralark” or TWAC for short.


Telling Our Story

As many of you may know, one of our goals for this server is to incorporate stories and lore into Coralark. While we are still working on how to implement that in a gameplay sense, we are taking our first step in the narrative direction with the introduction of lore cards. These will be short stories that might tell little side plots, unearth conspiracies, or just as a fun joke.

To house these, a new button on the yourmom.online homepage has opened and gives visitors access to all of the cards that have been released. At launch, three cards will be viewable. After that, we plan to release another three every week.

Make sure to go read them after you finish reading this blog. You might just stumble on some inspiration for your next project.
